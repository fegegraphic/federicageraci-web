About Me

I'm Federica, graphic designer specialized in illustration and Ui design. Graduated in graphic design at the Academy of Fine Arts in Catania, I finished a master's degree in Digital Communication in Rome which allowed me to join the creative team as an intern at the Leo Burnett advertising agency, within the Publicis group. I am currently following the Start2impact Ux / Ui Design course, after I finished the Ux / Ui design course by Grafigata.
